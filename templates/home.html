About KLP Roorkee

Kanhaiya Lal Polytechnic Roorkee

Kanhaiya Lal Polytechnic (KLP) Roorkee is a premier technical institution located in Roorkee, Haridwar district, Uttarakhand. The institute is affiliated with Uttarakhand Board of Technical Education (UBTER), Dehradun, and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi.

KLP is dedicated to providing quality technical education to students from Uttarakhand and surrounding regions. The college offers 3-year Diploma programs in various engineering disciplines, equipping students with the skills and knowledge required for industrial employment and entrepreneurship.

The institute has modern laboratories, workshops, a well-stocked library, and qualified faculty members who are committed to student success. KLP actively participates in campus placements, sports competitions, and cultural events, creating a holistic development environment for students.

Admission Process (JEEP)

Admissions are done through the Joint Entrance Exam for Polytechnics (JEEP) conducted by UBTER, Dehradun.

  1. Register online at ubter.in for JEEP exam
  2. Appear in the JEEP entrance examination
  3. Check results and merit rank on UBTER portal
  4. Participate in online counselling process
  5. Report to KLP Roorkee with original documents
  6. Complete admission formalities and fee payment
Visit UBTER Official Website
Eligibility
  • Passed 10th class (High School) from recognized board
  • Minimum 35% marks in aggregate
  • Science & Mathematics as compulsory subjects
  • Age: Min 14 years (relaxation for reserved categories)
Fee Structure
  • Tuition Fee as per UBTER norms
  • Examination Fee (per semester)
  • Development & Library Fee
  • Scholarship available for eligible students
Important Dates

  • Exam Date: Published on ubter.in
  • Result Declaration: After exam
  • Counselling: July–August (approx.)
